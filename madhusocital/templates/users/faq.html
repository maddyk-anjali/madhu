Frequently Asked Questions

What is Care One?

Care One is a platform designed to connect people with personalized health and wellness services.

Is Care One free to use?

Yes, the basic version is completely free. Premium features are available with a subscription.

How do I sign up?

Click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right and follow the instructions to create your account.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings.
