Government Schemes for Pregnant Women

Overview

The Government of India has introduced multiple welfare schemes aimed at supporting pregnant women. These initiatives ensure financial assistance, medical checkups, institutional deliveries, and proper nutrition to reduce maternal and infant mortality rates.

Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY)

Janani Suraksha Yojana(JSY)
